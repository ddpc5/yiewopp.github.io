08. Exercise: Data Visualizations in Product Proposals

Data Visualizations in Product Proposals Exercise Instructions

You and the user research team administered a survey to professionals from different industries inquiring if they are interested in creating an online course, and if so, why they haven’t done so. Here is how the survey was structured (each column corresponds an answer to the following questions):

  • Question 1: (Free response) What industry do you currently work in?
  • Question 2: (Free response) What is the title of your position?
  • Question 3: (Boolean) Have you ever created an online course in the past? (If yes, then - route to a different survey path)
  • Question 4: (Boolean) Have you ever wanted to create an online course? (If no, then route to a different survey path. If yes, then go to question 5 and onward)

Which of the following factors have prevented you from doing so?

  • Question 5: (Boolean) No software to efficiently create videos
  • Question 6: (Boolean) No hardware to efficiently create videos
  • Question 7: (Boolean) Lack of knowledge to properly teach the subject
  • Question 8: (Boolean) No content management system to create quizzes and exercises
  • Question 9: (Boolean) Lack of time to create a course
  • Question 10: (Boolean) Lack of knowledge on academic course design (grading rubric, curriculum, learning objectives)

Download this dataset & create visualizations to include in the product proposal deck to back up your statements.
