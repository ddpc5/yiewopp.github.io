Key Points to Hit in a Product Proposal Presentation Video Exercise Solution Summary

Here’s my solution:

Pain points

Subject matter expertise professionals want to spread their knowledge to online learners, but don’t know the proper way to set up a curriculum or facilitate course production (video editing).

Target Users

Professionals that are experts and passionate about their field, who want to share knowledge with others, but don’t have a background in academia and/or digital content creation.

User Impact

Providing professionals with a monetary side hustle, and a means of self-actualization on sharing their talents with anyone who wants to learn.

Providing students with the opportunity to learn about their desired skill sets from experts in the field, while maintaining near the same education quality as traditional academia, without being bound financially or geographically.

Market Impact

  • Supplement/complement to traditional academia.
  • Less strain/burden on the physical limitations of traditional academia.
  • Less personal strain on students not having to take on large student loan debts.

Business Impact

  • Scaling out course revenue, through an increased supply of courses.
  • Aligns with our 2020 goals.
    • Objective: Increase quality course production throughput at operational scale.

Solutions

An intuitive, self-serving LMS - Learning Management System - for instructors to input, publish, and manage their written and video content.

Risks & Assumptions

  • Recording hardware limitations
    • Shipping service with instructions.
  • Coding environments
    • Instructors to set criterion of success for students.

Launch Strategy

  • Experiments on each iteration with a batch of early adopter instructors, with higher commission.
  • Template-heavy initially.
