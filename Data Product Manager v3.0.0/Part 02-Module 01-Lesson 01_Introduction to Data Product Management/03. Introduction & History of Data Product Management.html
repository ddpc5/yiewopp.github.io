Introduction & History of Data Product Management Summary

Data in the real-world can oftentimes be extremely large (to the point where Excel spreadsheets, visualization tools, or relational databases cannot process), erroneous, partial (missing key slices), biased, undocumented, and/or inaccessible.

What is data product management? Generally, one of the three below areas (and sometimes a combination):

  • Building data products
    • Netflix's content recommendation engine
    • Spotify's Year in Review
    • Mint’s Budgeting Dashboard
  • Building data platforms to enable data scientists & analysts to access, store, analyze, enrich, transform, aggregate, encrypt, visualize, and/or deliver data
  • Leveraging data science to make stronger decisions as a product manager from defining the minimum viable product to iterative design & experimentation
