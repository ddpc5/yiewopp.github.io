02. Prerequisites

Prerequisites Heading

Course Prerequisites

ND030 C1 L0 A02 Prerequisites

Prerequisites Summary

This course is best suited for:

  • a data scientist, data analyst, or data engineer yearning to apply their skills towards product management
  • a product manager that wants to become more data-driven in their decision making
  • or just anyone who’s eager to learn the fundamentals of data product management through hands-on projects

The recommended prerequisites for the course are:

  • basic knowledge of SQL
  • basic knowledge of Tableau or any drag-&-drop business intelligence tool, like or not limited to Mode Analytics, Periscope Data, Looker, Qlik Sense, or Spotfire
