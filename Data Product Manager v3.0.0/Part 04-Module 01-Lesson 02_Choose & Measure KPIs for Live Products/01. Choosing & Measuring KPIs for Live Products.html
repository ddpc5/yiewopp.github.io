Choosing & Measuring KPIs for Live Products

ND030 C3 L1 01 Choosing & Measuring Metrics For Live Products- Video

Summary

In this lesson, we will talk about choosing and calculating key performance indicators (KPI) for products that are live in the field. Key Performance Indicators, are the metrics that you’ve selected as the most important metrics to track for your products. At the end of the day, we want to build successful products and by using measurable KPIs, product managers can quantify the level of success of their products.
