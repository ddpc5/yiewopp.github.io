06. KPIs for Different Business Models Quizzes

KPIs for Different Business Models Quizzes

eCommerce Business Model KPIs

Select all the KPIs that would be a good fit for an eCommerce business model.
SOLUTION:
  • Number of purchases
  • User satisfaction ratings
  • Average profit per sale

Software as a Service Business Model KPIs

Select all the KPIs that would be a good fit for a Software as a Service (SAAS) business model.
SOLUTION:
  • User Satisfaction ratings
  • Percent of users who go from free trial to paid subscription
  • Number of purchasers or subscribers

Freemium Business Model KPIs

Select all the KPIs that would be a good fit for a freemium business model.
SOLUTION:
  • Percent of users who go from free user to paid subscriber
  • Percent of users who pay for upgrades to the base (free) product
  • User Satisfaction ratings
