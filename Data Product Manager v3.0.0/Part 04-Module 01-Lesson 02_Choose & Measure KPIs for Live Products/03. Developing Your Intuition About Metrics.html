Developing Your Intuition About Metrics

ND030 C3 L1 03 Developing Your Intuition About Metrics Video

Summary

Google researchers created a framework called the HEART (H-E-A-R-T) model to help you examine different types of metrics. The heart model simplifies the vast amounts of data you have by categorizing them into five categories: Happiness, Engagement, Adoption, Retention, Task.

  • Happiness: how satisfied a customer is with a product or feature
  • Engagement: the frequency with which users interact with a product or feature. A "good" engagement varies by product. Some products are used daily but others are used annually.
  • ** Adoption**: the number of *new users* who begin to use a product.
  • ** Retention**: how many customers *come back* to a product.
  • ** Task**: how *easy* a product is for customers to use.
