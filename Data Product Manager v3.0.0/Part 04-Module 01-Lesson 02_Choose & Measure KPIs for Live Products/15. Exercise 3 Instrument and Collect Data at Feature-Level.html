Instrument and Collect Data at Feature-Level Exercise

Instrumenting a New Referral Feature

You work at a food delivery startup "Foodfast" that sends pre-made meals to customers on a weekly basis. Customers pay per week for each food delivery and can skip a delivery at any time for no charge. You've convinced your CEO of the importance and power of referrals for attracting new customers. In the next week, your development team will be deploying a test of a new feature that allows current users to send a referral code to friends. If the friend joins the service, they get 50% off their first purchase (so that the first purchase simply covers the net cost of the food/packing/delivery to your company). At this time, the referrer gets no bonus. But in the future, to increase the incentive to refer friends, the company has a plan to experiment with offering a discount off the referrer's next order if the referred user signs up for a full-price delivery.
