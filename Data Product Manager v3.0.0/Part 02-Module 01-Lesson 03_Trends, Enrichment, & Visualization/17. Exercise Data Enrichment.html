17. Exercise: Data Enrichment

Product managers are nowadays even present in the tech entities of governing parties. Let’s say you're the product manager for the digital arm of a politician registered as an independent. After some research, you and your team have decided that one of the campaigns you will run will target folks who buy tickets online to American football events.

Given that this politician's budget is fairly limited, you’ve decided to stick to campaigns in the Northeast region of the United States, where this candidate's awareness is relatively lower than other regions. You will need to make a decision on which football teams’ audiences to target, based on this user research survey data. The dataset is also available on GitHub.

Finding additional data

Looking at the provided dataset, you determine that you don’t quite have all the information you need. You decide to find a dataset online that you can use to enrich your user research data. What type of dataset would be most helpful for you to look for?
SOLUTION: A linking of US states to regions

Data Cleaning

Remember, once you have located a dataset containing the information you need, you may need to clean the dataset. This is a common first step that data product managers often need to complete as part of finding trends. Use this exercise as practice for that skill.

Visualizing the data

After cleaning and joining your datasets, you want to start identifying your target audience. What type of visualization will be most helpful to you?
SOLUTION: A bar chart of independent political affiliation by team

Make a recommendation

QUESTION:

Now that you’ve decided on a visualization type, you can go ahead and construct your chart in Tableau. You decide to choose two teams whose fans and audience you will target for the politician’s campaign. Which two teams do you recommend for the campaign and why?

ANSWER:

By looking at which football teams in the Northeast have the most fans who identify politically as independents, we can see that our campaign will likely be the most effective by targeting fans of the New England Patriots and the Pittsburgh Steelers.
