06. Exercise (cont): Line Graphs & Pie Charts

You’ve decided to look at data on seasonal bookings trends and bookings split by hotel type. Now it's time to start visualizing the data.

Visualizing bookings trends

What visualization would be best to look at seasonal bookings trends?
SOLUTION: A line graph showing rooms booked by month

Visualizing hotel type split

What visualization would be best to look at the split of hotel type?
SOLUTION: A pie chart showing percentages of bookings by hotel type

Identifying seasonal trends

Construct your visualizations in Tableau and take a look at the resulting data. Which month has the highest volume of hotel bookings?
SOLUTION: August

Hotel type

Which type of hotel is more common for bookings?
SOLUTION: City hotels

Make a recommendation

QUESTION:

Based on the competitor data you have observed, make a recommendation to your executive team about what time of year and type of region your company should target for the launch of its online marketplace, and explain the reasoning behind your recommendation.

ANSWER:

Great work! Some factors to think about here are when your competitors are most and least busy (which indicates when guests are most likely to be traveling and in need of a place to stay), and what type of hotel is more commonly booked (which indicates where people are more likely to be traveling to).
