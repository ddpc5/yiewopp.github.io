13. Quiz: Box Plots & Histograms

Box Plots and Histograms Quiz 1

QUIZ QUESTION::

Match the below definitions to their related terms.

ANSWER CHOICES:



Definition

Term

Visualization effective at displaying the spread of data, its clusters, its outliers, and its range.

Visualization effective at comparing bins (or ranges) of continuous variables.

Visualization effective at comparing discrete variables.

SOLUTION:

Definition

Term

Visualization effective at comparing discrete variables.

Visualization effective at displaying the spread of data, its clusters, its outliers, and its range.

Visualization effective at comparing bins (or ranges) of continuous variables.

You're a growth PM at analyzing the aspects of an engaged, repeating user. You want to segment users by transaction amount, and look at existing retention rates of each segment individually. You’re seeking to visualize how many users on Venmo transact $0 - $10 per month, how many transact $10 - $20 per month, and so forth.

What would be the best visualization to display this information?
SOLUTION: Histogram
