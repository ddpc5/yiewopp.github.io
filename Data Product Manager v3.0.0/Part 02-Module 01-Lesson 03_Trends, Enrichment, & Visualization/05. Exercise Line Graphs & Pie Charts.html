05. Exercise: Line Graphs & Pie Charts

Rewind the clock, and let’s say you’re back in 2003. You’re the product manager at a company that is starting up an online marketplace to match visitors with hosts for temporary homestays. Your executive team is interested in hearing your thoughts about when and where to launch the marketplace.

Competitor research

You plan to start by doing some research on potential competitors. Who are your competitors most likely to be?
SOLUTION: Hotels

Once you have identified who you are likely to be competing with, you can start to look at data from those competitors. This will help you make informed decisions for your company as you get closer to the launch of your product.

Competitor data

What competitor data will be of interest to your executive team?
SOLUTION:
  • Competitor sub-type (e.g. resort vs city)
  • Seasonal bookings trends
