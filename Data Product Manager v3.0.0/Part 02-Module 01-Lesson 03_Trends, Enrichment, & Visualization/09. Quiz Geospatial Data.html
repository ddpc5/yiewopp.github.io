09. Quiz: Geospatial Data

Geospatial Data Quiz 1

QUIZ QUESTION::

Match the below definitions to the related term.

ANSWER CHOICES:



Definition

Term

Display the frequency of geospatial point data on a discrete scale, based on what boundary you specify

Display the frequency of geospatial point data on a continuous scale
SOLUTION:

Definition

Term

Display the frequency of geospatial point data on a discrete scale, based on what boundary you specify

Display the frequency of geospatial point data on a continuous scale

You're a PM for a bike sharing company where users rent bikes from stations throughout various cities. This quarter, you're looking to launch a monthly membership in the U.S. (the service is already in all 50 states.) You start your analysis by determining which states have the highest ride-per-capita ratios. Which geospatial visualization would you use to conduct your analyses?
SOLUTION: Cloropleth map

Visualizations: Geospatial Data Hard Quiz 2

You noticed that Ohio was one of the states with the highest ride-per-capita ratios, and your team decided to focus there. How would you determine which zip codes have the highest volume of rides?
SOLUTION: Choropleth map

Visualizations: Geospatial Data Easy Quiz 3

You noticed that Columbus, OH had the highest density, so you focus there. You'll want to survey users, how do you determine which areas of the city have the highest pick-up / drop-off activity?
SOLUTION: Density map
