06. Exercise 1: User Conversion Funnels

User Conversion Funnels Exercise: Online Shopping Experience

Instruction

We can learn a lot from our own experiences with products. For example, if you've ever purchased an item online from an e-commerce site for the first time, you went through a user conversion funnel. In this exercise, you'll list the stages and steps as you went through a conversion funnel when purchasing an item online.

Example

Here is an example of what the awareness stage and steps of your shopping funnel might look like. The example chosen is a funnel that ends in the purchase of a dog toy from an online store.

Stage: Awareness

Step(s): The customer learns about the website, perhaps while searching for a particular type of item. Paid advertising can also come into play here via Adwords and other marketing techniques.

Example: when Nina searches on Google for "toys for dog chewy" she sees an ad for DogToys.com

List Stages and Steps

QUESTION:

Name the stages and list the steps of the user acquisition and activation funnel you went through in a recent online purchase. Provide an example under each step. For bonus points, while going through stages and steps, make suggestions about how the online shopping funnel you experienced could have been improved!

(If you never make online purchases, feel free to do this exercise while thinking of an in-person acquisition/activation funnel.)
