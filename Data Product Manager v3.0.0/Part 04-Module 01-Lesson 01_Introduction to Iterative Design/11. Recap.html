ND030 C3 L0 10 Recap Video

Recap

Congratulations! You have just finished the first lesson of this course

In this lesson, we first learned about the design-implement-evaluate cycle of iterative design and the design thinking cycle of empathize-define-ideate-prototype-test-implement.

Then, we covered many business stakeholders a data product manager needs to work with to find success in their role.

In the third part of the lesson, we learned about the history of iterative design, especially its roots in software development and the spiral development model.

In the last part of this lesson, we talked about the final project for the course. In the project, you will act as a data product manager to evaluate the performance of a live app and analyze its data to determine the best next step in a cycle of iterative design.
