07. Business Stakeholders

Business Stakeholders

ND030 C3 L0 07 Business Stakeholders Video

Summary

There are many teams that you will be closely working with as a product manager. These teams are:

  • Finance: Finance leads will help you measure KPIs that tie into the financial health of your product.
  • Marketing: Marketing sells your product to potential customers. They have a great interest in the things you plan to change, upgrade, or add to a product. In exchange, the marketing or sales team will provide feedback from real customers and great insight into the competitive landscape.
  • Design: Designers often help with creating simple prototypes for testing as well as leading usability testing on more finalized designs.
  • Data science: Data scientists run deep analyses to help data product managers understand how to better meet user needs.
  • Engineering: Engineers build and make changes to a product based on the direction of product management. They also help instrument products and features so that data can be collected.

As a product manager, you should understand the function of each team and how they will affect the product or the product design process.
