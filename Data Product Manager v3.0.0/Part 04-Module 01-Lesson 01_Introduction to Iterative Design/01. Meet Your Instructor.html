Meet Your Instructor

ND030 C3 L0 01 Meet Your Instructor Video

Hello and welcome!

My name is Anne Rynearson.

I have almost a decade of experience as a software product manager, including managing and launching consumer and enterprise-facing products in the education and market research industries. My focus is on developing Agile teams that use data-driven decision making as part of a cycle of iterative product development.

I’m excited to share my knowledge with you and hope you will enjoy the journey of exploring data-driven iterative product design with me.

Fun Facts

Professional accomplishments aside…here are some fun facts about me!

  • I have two cats named Zahara and Nico (who luckily didn't interrupt my video shoots for this course too often!)
  • I'm originally from New Hampshire, but currently live and work in Los Angeles
  • I drink cups and cups of tea every day
