08. Exercise 1: Integrate Qualitative Data into Iterative Design

Integrate Qualitative Data into Iterative Design Exercise

Instruction

Read the following scenario about a hypothetical online wedding planning platform and provide suggestions about how and when qualitative data might be used.

Scenario: An online wedding planning platform notices that their users meet and book many photographers through the platform, but not many caterers. The product managers of the platform assume that this is because photographers' portfolios can be reviewed online, but couples might want to taste a caterers' food in person. The product management team is brainstorming on how to increase the booking of caterers.
