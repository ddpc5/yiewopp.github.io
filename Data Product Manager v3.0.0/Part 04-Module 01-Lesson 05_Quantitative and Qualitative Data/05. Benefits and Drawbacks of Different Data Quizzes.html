Qualitative Data

SOLUTION:

In the following scenario, which type of data would be the most important to gather?

Scenario: A product manager just launched an app that allows people to convert recipes into grocery lists and then order food delivery based on the grocery list. There are lots of users converting recipes, but only a very small percentage (3%) then go on to buy groceries. The product manager wants to know why.
