Qualitative Research Methods Quizzes

QUIZ QUESTION::

Match the type of qualitative user research with the description of what a product manager did

ANSWER CHOICES:



Qualitative Research Type

Product Manager Actions

Usability Testing

User Interviews

Focus Group
SOLUTION:

Qualitative Research Type

Product Manager Actions

User Interviews

Usability Testing

Focus Group

User Interviews

In the following scenario, what are the reasons that it might be best to conduct user interviews instead of a focus group?

Scenario: a product manager for a women's health app wants to better understand why women track their periods and sex life in the app. This is exploratory research with no deadline, but the product manager knows it will be helpful for empathizing with the users.
SOLUTION:
  • The topic you want to discuss with customers is sensitive and users might be uncomfortable discussing it in front of users
  • You want to ask lots of follow up questions and dig into each user's background and motivations
