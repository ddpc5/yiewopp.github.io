Mapping business goals to data consumer groups

  • A working product that will be the focus of the Engineering Team
  • Getting new customers and retaining the old ones, the Marketing Team will work towards that
  • Predicting Profit and loss growth is taken care of by the Finance Team
  • Product Delivery will be taken care of by our Logistics team
  • Having sufficient inventory is the objective of the Inventory Management Team
  • Addressing customer grievances, Customer Care Team will do that
  • Improving the product is Product Management Team's work
