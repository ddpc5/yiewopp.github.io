Data Use-cases Recap

Broadly, data use-cases can be categorized into 5 buckets:

  1. Business Intelligence
  2. Visualization
  3. Reporting
  4. Big-data
  5. ML

Below are example use-cases for each category.

Business Intelligence use-cases would be:

  • Understand your customers more effectively
  • Drive performance and revenue
  • Identify sales trends
  • Provide personalized service more easily
  • Improve operational efficiency

Visualization use-cases are:

  • Identifying trends and spikes.
  • Tracking metrics
  • Getting notified when changes occur
  • Real-time monitoring
  • Accessing data displays remotely

Reporting use-cases would be:

  • Public reporting of operating and financial data
  • Customer reports
  • Reconciliation reports
  • Reports to decision-makers
  • Reports for business reviews – monthly business reviews

Big Data use-cases could be:

  • Fraud Prevention
  • Price Optimization
  • Recommendation Engine
  • Social Media Analysis
  • Complete Customer view

ML use-cases are:

  • Optimum resource utilization
  • Diagnosing ailments and creating treatment plans
  • Virtual assistants: Alexa, Siri, Google, etc
  • Personalized customer experiences: Disney, etc
  • Content creation: Netflix, etc
