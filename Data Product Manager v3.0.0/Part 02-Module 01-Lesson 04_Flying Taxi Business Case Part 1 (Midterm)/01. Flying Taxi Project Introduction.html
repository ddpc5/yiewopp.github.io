01. Flying Taxi Project Introduction

Bringing the First Flying Car Taxi Service to Market

In this project, you will apply the skills acquired in this course to strategize how to unveil the first flying car taxi service, Flyber, in one of the most congested cities in America-- New York City. You are responsible for bringing the first flying car taxi service to market by analyzing data in Part 1 (Midterm) and building a product proposal in Part 2 (Final).

Your data scientist has passed you a plethora of visualizations, model outputs and aggregated statistics from a dataset containing real taxi drop-offs and pick-ups in New York City. Because Flyber is the first flying car, your team has acquired taxi data for a comparable initial analysis.

You will be using the provided slide deck template (or Powerpoint version) to complete your Business Proposal and answer the provided question, and will also be provided a SQL workspace to be able to work with the data. You may use any presentation tool (Google Slides, Microsoft Powerpoint, etc.) and upload the file through the workspace.
