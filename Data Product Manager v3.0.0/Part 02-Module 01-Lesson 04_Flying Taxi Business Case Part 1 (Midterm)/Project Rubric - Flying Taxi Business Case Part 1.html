Flying Taxi Business Case Part 1

PM Basics

Criteria Meet Specification

The project outputs the existing use cases of taxis, their users, their shortcomings with the existing product, and hypotheses around how a flying taxi car service will solve these problems.

The project answers the questions on the product and market from a fundamental, atomic, objective standpoint.

The project forms hypotheses that not only address user issues, but market-centric issues, including how a flying taxi service would affect the driver experience & traffic conditions.

Data Exploration

Criteria Meet Specification

The project outputs an understanding of the high-level granularity and scope of a dataset.

The project identifies the number of records, what each record represents, the primary key of the dataset, and the temporal & spatial ranges of the dataset.

The project outputs an understanding of the distribution and scope of the critical dimensions in the dataset.

The project identifies the average, median, and 1st/2nd standard deviations of key dimensions within the dataset.

Data Visualization

Criteria Meet Specification

The project outputs an understanding of spatial analyses in the dataset.

The project identifies the spatial trends in historical taxi ride dataset to identify viable locations for the flying taxi service pick-up & drop-off nodes, through plotting coordinates on a geospatial map, visualizing density, and overlaying with third-party data sources.

The project outputs an understanding of temporal analyses in the dataset.

The project identifies temporal trends in the dataset through time-series visualizations, in order to to understand when the optimal hours and/or days of week to deploy the flying taxi car service.

User Research Analysis

Criteria Meet Specification

The project analyzes user research data to further support or reject the product proposal.

The project identifies insights around qualitative responses around the existing use cases for ride-share ground transportation, if people would be willing to use a flying taxi car service, how much they are willing to pay for it, various personas of potential adopters, and general sentiment around the concern/excitement of such a service.

Tips to make your project standout:

  • The project identifies additional data streams that might help with the next stages of the rollout.
  • The project provides a working link to the Public Tableau data and visualizations.
