KS DP components

Inputs

Data Origin

To recap our Data origin points would be our website, app, SaaS Solutions, and our connected backpacks.

Data Storage

We have two types of data storage,

  • Traditional databases that store structured or processed data for our business professionals. - Data lakes to store structured, semi-structured, and unstructured data for our data scientists.

Data Destination.

The application that the data scientist team is using for ML modeling and the business intelligence software that the business team is using for business insights are our data destinations

The new group that we have introduced in the last few concepts is our Saas solutions. We will feed data to these solutions for accounting and for creating marketing campaigns. These are also our destinations.
