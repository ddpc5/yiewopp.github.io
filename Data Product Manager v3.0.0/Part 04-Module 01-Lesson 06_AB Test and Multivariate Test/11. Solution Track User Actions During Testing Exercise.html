11. Solution: Track User Actions During Testing Exercise

Solution: Track User Actions During Testing Exercise

ND030 C3 L5 08 Track User Actions During Testing Exercise Solution Video

Summary

Scenario 1 :

  • Business A needs to track the number of unique visitors to the page where they will be testing the new design.
  • They will need to track whether a user sees the control or the experimental design on the page.
  • They will also likely want to track the device and browser that the visitor is using in case the designs perform differently across device types.
  • Finally, to know which page is more effective, they will need to track whether the visitor takes the desired action of using the social linking feature

Scenario 2:

  • The business will need to track the number of pictures, which pieces of artwork, and in which order, are shown to the visitors.
  • The business will also need to track visitors (such as by dropping a cookie) to ensure the visitors are first-time visitors since that is the audience in question.
  • The business will want to track device type and OS in case different designs do better on different screen sizes.
  • Finally, given that the business has stated its goal is to prompt more visitors to click through the landing page to learn more information about the business, the business will need to track whether a specific visitor clicks through for more information in order to determine the winning condition.
