Benefits and Drawbacks of A/B and Multivariate Testing

Which of the following are true statements about the benefits and drawbacks of A/B and/or multivariate tests?
SOLUTION:
  • Multivariate test allows product managers to see how different changes impact each other
  • A/B and multivariate test both allow product managers to measure the impact of a product change on user conversion

A product manager wants to make 4 changes to a feature's design (the color of the page, the color of the submit button, the picture at the top of the page, and the word on the submit button). For each change, there are 2 new options (different than the current design). How many different test designs will the product manager have during a multivariate experiment?
SOLUTION: 81 test versions, including the control
