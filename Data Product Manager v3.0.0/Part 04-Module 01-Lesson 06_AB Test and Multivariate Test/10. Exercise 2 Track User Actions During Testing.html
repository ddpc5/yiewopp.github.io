Exercise 2: Track User Actions During Testing

Instruction

Now the two businesses, business A and business B need help in deciding what data to track during the experiments. Read the scenarios again below and explain what data should be tracked if it isn't already. Specifically call out what data will help the business determine if a test condition wins over the control.

Scenario #1

QUESTION:

Business A has a page accessible only to existing users. They have a new and different design for the page to use a specific feature that allows user to link their social accounts. They have a variety of colors and images they would love to test on the page. They do note that the business is fairly new and has a small number of users right now. Given your advice, they'll be running an A/B test with the existing design and one version of the new design being shown to different users.

Which data should be tracked on the page if it isn't already? Which data will determine the winning condition of the page?

QUESTION:

Given your advice, they'll be running a multivariate test that shows a different number of pictures and different pieces of artwork on the new visitor landing page.
