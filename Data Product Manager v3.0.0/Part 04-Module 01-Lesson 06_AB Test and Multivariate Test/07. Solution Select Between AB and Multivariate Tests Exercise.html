07. Solution: Select Between A/B and Multivariate Tests Exercise

Solution: Select Between A/B and Multivariate Tests Exercise

ND030 C3 L5 06 Choosing Between A-B And Multivariate Tests Exercise Solution Video

Summary

Scenario 1 :

The business should use an A/B test on the new design of the page. With a small number of users, it would take a long time to receive enough visitors to gain conclusive evidence on the best direction to take the page if they presented a multivariate test with many colors and images. Even though they could run a multivariate test in the sense of testing multiple variations of the page, the business should stick with an A/B test given the traffic the page in question receives.

Scenario 2:

The business should use a multivariate test on the design of the page. With a large number of new users landing on the page, the business has the traffic to come to a conclusion even when testing multiple options and variations. The business can use dynamic tools to adjust the number of pictures shown on the site to determine the number of pictures both across and down the page that result in the best click through. As a next step, they can also test different images in different spots to conclude which generates the most visitor interest.
