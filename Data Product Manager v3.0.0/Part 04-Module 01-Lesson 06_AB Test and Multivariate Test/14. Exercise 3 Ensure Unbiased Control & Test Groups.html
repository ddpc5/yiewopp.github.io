14. Exercise 3: Ensure Unbiased Control & Test Groups

Exercise 3: Ensure Unbiased Control & Test Groups

Instruction

The two businesses, business A and business B need more help from you. With your testing expertise, you let them know whether they would be better running an A/B test or a multivariate test and what data to track. Now the business needs help in deciding which users to place into control and test groups for their experiments. Read the scenarios again below and explain how to create unbiased control and test groups for these scenarios. Specifically call out what user elements might be important to balance among control and test groups.

Scenario #1

QUESTION:

Business A has a page accessible only to existing users. They have a new and different design for the page to push these existing users to take a desired action. They have a variety of colors and images they would love to test on the page. They do note that the business is fairly new and has a small number of users right now. The business will be running an A/B test. Given your advice, they'll be running an A/B test with the existing design and one version of the new design being shown to different users.

How should users be separated into control and test groups? Are there differences in the type of user that should be balanced among control and test groups?

Scenario #2

QUESTION:

Business B is a gallery with a landing page for new users that shows a number of pictures of artwork. They get a lot of traffic and are trying to figure out how many and which pictures to show on the page to convince users the site is useful without overwhelming them. They want to know the ideal number of pictures (and which pictures) will prompt the most new users to click through for more information about the business. Given your advice, they'll be running a multivariate test that shows a different number of pictures and different pieces of artwork on the new visitor landing page.

How should users be separated into control and test groups? Are there differences in the type of user that should be balanced among control and test groups?
