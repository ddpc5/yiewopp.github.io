17. Quiz: Designing Features that Gather Explicit Feedback

You work for a company that has created an app that lets users create and post short video stories. As the PM for the creator experience, you want to gather feedback from video creators using the app that can help you determine if your app is successful for video creators or not.

Type of feedback

What type of feedback might be useful to gather from video creators using the app?
SOLUTION:
  • How the experience is of uploading a video
  • Ease of titling/tagging a video
  • Usefulness of editing functionality

Thought Exercise

QUESTION:

Now that you've thought a little about what type of feedback you might be interested in gathering from video creators using the app, what are some specific features that you could implement in the app to collect that feedback to help you determine if your product is successful for video creators or not?

ANSWER:

Nice work! An example of a feature you could implement to gather feedback from video creators could be a "flag" that the user could toggle if they experienced difficulty in uploading a video. This is just one example - you might have thought of something totally different, and that's okay! The important thing is being able to explain your thought process and justify your reasoning.
