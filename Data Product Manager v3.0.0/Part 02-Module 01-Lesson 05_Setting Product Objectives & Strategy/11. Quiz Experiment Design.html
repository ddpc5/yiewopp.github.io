Experiment Design Thought Exercise

QUESTION:

You are the product manager for a social media platform. This quarter you are focusing on driving activation/acquisition of smartwatch users by experimenting with how smartwatch videos are displayed on users' feeds.

Guide us through how you would design an experiment that changes how smartwatch videos are displayed on the feed. What would your hypothesis be? What would the experimental variant be? Would this be a client- or server-side experiment? How big should the sample size be, so that the experiment could garner statistically significant results?

ANSWER:

Nice work!
There are a lot of ways to approach this scenario. For example, you might hypothesize that smartwatch users will be more engaged with your social media platform if smartwatch videos are highlighted in their feeds. To test this hypothesis, you could run a client-side experiment to compare your current feed with a new feature that puts a colorful border around smartwatch video posts in users' feeds. Again, there are lots of ways you could approach this scenario - this example is by no means the only way!
