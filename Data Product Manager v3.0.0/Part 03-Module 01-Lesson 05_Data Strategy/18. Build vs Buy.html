Build vs Buy

When to Build

When should you build?

  • When time commitments are critical, and vendors are not able to honor that.
  • You should build if the total cost of building, including development, testing, configuration, and scaling, along with talent and infrastructure, is much lower.
  • If a deep understanding of your business is needed.
  • You should also build when the costs of customization are huge.
  • It is always best to build for reasons of strategic importance that might provide you a competitive advantage.

When To Buy

When should you buy?

  • If it will take you too long to build.
  • When the overall cost, including Licensing, Implementation, Integration, Customization, support, and infrastructure is much lower.
  • It is always good to buy market-available solutions for problems that are common across enterprises. For example, HR solutions.
  • If the problems have already been solved and there are market leaders available with solutions. For example, buying cloud storage on AWS or on Azure would be better than building it.
  • Buying some of the tech solutions will help you scale more quickly and reduce your speed to market.
